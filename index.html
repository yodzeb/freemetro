how to play

connect. drag from one station to another to start a line. drag from a line endpoint to extend.

deliver. passengers (small shapes) want the station shape they wear. trains pick up and drop along your lines.

survive. if a station stays overcrowded too long, it's game over. tap a station to inspect.

edit. drag the small grip on a line endpoint to reroute (not in extreme mode). long-press a line segment to delete the line.

cross water. tunnels & bridges are required to cross rivers. they are limited.

weekly. at the start of each week you choose an upgrade: a new line, a train, a carriage, an interchange, or tunnels.
