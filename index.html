how to play

connect. drag from one station to another to start a line. each line has its own colour.

extend. every line endpoint shows a small grip in the line's colour — drag it to a new station to extend, or drag it back onto the previous station on the line to shrink that segment off.

deliver. passengers (small shapes) want to reach a station of their shape. they board any train whose line — directly or by transfer — leads to such a station. each delivery pays one coin (two for exotic shapes).

spend. open the depot (coin button or B) to buy extra lines, trains, carriages, interchanges and crossings. each purchase makes the next of its kind cost more.

remove a line. click the line's coloured chip in the bottom tray. its trains, carriages and crossings return to inventory.

finish. normal mode is won at 500 deliveries; extreme at 750. ridership grows as your score climbs. endless and creative have no win or lose. after a win, you can keep building.

survive. if a station stays overcrowded for too long, it's game over. plan transfers to spread the load.

cross water. rivers need crossings; you start with three. buy more in the depot.
